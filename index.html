Now imagine a tool designed for moments when a child's familiar route changes.

Kidova is more than a location tracker. It combines a child's familiar journey with city and street-level information, such as transit changes, road conditions, risky crossings, weather, and nearby safe places. It gives the child one clear next step while helping their grown-up understand what is happening around them and decide how to provide support.
Privacy promise: This event prototype does not ask for a child's name, school, home address, or live location.
